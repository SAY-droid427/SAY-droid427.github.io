The Streets Of Kolkata

Art does not always depict beauty and the beautiful sceneries. It depicts the lives of common people, the masses.
this painting is done in water-colors and depicts the narrow, dusty streets of the slums of the City Of Joy-Kolkata-my homeland.Kolkata is the land of the great poets,the greatest brains of my country-India.

Sundarbans

This digital artwork done in Krita is purely inspired by the pain and agony of the people affected by the super cyclone-Amphan.Sundarbans, the largest mangrove , the beauty of India and the pride of Bengal was badly affected by Amphan, that bereaved the people of their homes and their fields, their farmlands.Corona had taken away their living and now Amphan had taken away their lives.Sundarbans is known for The Royal Bengal Tiger.So I had depicted the tigers in the helpless situation surrounded by water.
