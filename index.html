About

Hi there, this is Sayani Mallick!



Hi! I'm an accomplished final year undergraduate student at BITS PILANI, K.K. Birla Goa campus, with a strong proficiency in computer science fundamentals. My primary passion lies in software development and machine learning. I recently completed a successful 2-month summer internship at Flipkart, where I made valuable contributions to the M3 Data Apps Team by creating a new feature for the Brand Insights Tool: New vs. Repeat customer Metrics. In addition, I have been recognized as an Outreachy intern at Eclipse Adoptium, where I played a pivotal role in revamping the external tests process by reducing repetitive code and restructuring the testing approach. I also had the honor of being a speaker at EclipseCON 2021, where I presented my project on "Having Fun with Testing".

As a versatile researcher, I have a keen interest in deep learning and computer vision, and have even ventured into the field of reinforcement learning. My research paper on "Sleep Apnea Detection on Resource-Constrained Devices using Machine Learning" has been accepted for publication in SysCON 2023, showcasing my expertise in leveraging machine learning for healthcare applications. With my extensive experience in software development, machine learning, and research, I am driven to make a meaningful impact in the field of computer science and technology.
