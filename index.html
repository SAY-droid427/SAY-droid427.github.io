About

Hi there, this is Sayani Mallick!

I am a final year student of Birla Institute Of Technology and Sciences, Pilani(Goa Campus), currently pursuing my BE in Electronics and Communications Engineering.

I am a software developer with a great interest in data science. I am also a competitive programmer coding mainly in C++. I love working on machine learning problems. I love working with data and have experience in handling Big Data. The impact that data creates is very inspiring to me.

I have worked as a software developer intern at Flipkart in the Data Apps Team in the summer of 2022 where I have handled voluminous data to create positive impact on the shopping platform. I have also worked as an Outreachy intern with Eclipse Adoptium in the summer of 2021. I have also worked at several startups to gain hands-on knowledge of software development.

My hobbies include painting, listening to music, reading and coding. I love mentoring juniors who need guidance in open source, software development or coding.
