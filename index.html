About

Hey, this is Sayani Mallick!



I am a software development engineer working at Flipkart, Bangalore, India.

I have received my BE degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from BITS Pilani, KK Birla Goa Campus. I am also a researcher with interest in the fields of computer vision, deep learning and signal processing, especially for applications in the field of healthcare. I have also worked as a research intern in IIIT Delhi, where I have worked on deep learning models for sensor calibration.
