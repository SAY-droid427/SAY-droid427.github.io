I am Sayani Mallick! I hail from West Bengal. I have spent my childhood in Odisha and Jharkhand. I have completed my Class XII th from Ranchi, Jharkhand.I am currently pursuing my Engineering in Electronics and Communication from BITS Pilani, KK Birla Goa Campus.

I am also an artist in Kala,the Fine Arts Society of BITS GOA.
I believe that "Every child is an artist. But it all lies in how we maintain that art when we are adults".Hope you will like my artwork.Please feel free to reach out to me for discussing art,engineering and coffee!
