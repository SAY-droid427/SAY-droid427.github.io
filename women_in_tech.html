A Request

This is a earnest request to you, reader! If you are a woman, then I urge you to be confident. I urge you to be ambitious. I urge you to speak up and say 'yes' to every opportunity you get to improve yourself. I urge you to be proud of yourself. Next time you see a woman being torn down, be her support. Next time, you see a hackathon being scheduled throughout ht enight, urge the organizers to change the time so that women who feel unsafe and are not allowed to stay outside home during nights can also particpate. Next time you see a girl having the 'learned technological helplessness', tell her that she can do it. And above all, if this website helped you in any way, empower another woman by guiding her, by mentoring her. You can do it.
If you are a man, I urge you to realize that at home, it is your responsibility to complete half of the household chores. It is not just a woman's job. I urge you to realize that when in a meeting, a woman is quiet, she might be feeling at unease because she is the only woman in the room. I urge you to make the environment safe for her. I urge you to be a man who empowers woman.
I urge everyone to realize that next time you see a little girl staring at the computer in wonder,they need to encourage her to go on and switch on the computer. I urge you all to encourage women in tech.
